What to Prepare

A stronger application starts with clear information.

Exact requirements vary by lender and situation, but most applications need a clean view of income, expenses, debts, deposit, identity, and property details.

  • Recent income evidence and employment details.
  • Bank statements, existing loan details, and living expense information.
  • Deposit source, purchase timeline, and property or build information.
  • Any KiwiSaver first-home withdrawal process questions can be discussed as general process guidance.
